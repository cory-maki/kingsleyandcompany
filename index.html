Who We Are

Real Estate With a Purpose

Kingsley Consulting, doing business as Kingsley + Co., connects individuals and organizations to form strategic partnerships and develop innovative business solutions that revitalize communities. Chinedum Ndukwe founded the firm to bring disciplined investment and development expertise to neighborhoods that are too often overlooked by conventional capital.

Kingsley + Co. specializes in identifying lucrative opportunities and assembling teams to invest and execute projects through stabilization and beyond, offering redevelopment, asset management, leasing, and design & build services under one roof.

That mission is most visible in projects like the firm's Walnut Hills senior housing development, which brought urgently needed affordable units to a community that had gone without new investment for years.
